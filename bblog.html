My Exprience

Women In Finance

"In the Bloomberg Women in Finance program, I gained valuable insights into finance while drawing inspiration from the remarkable women who guided me throughout this transformative journey. On the inaugural day, Ashima Ma'am shared profound wisdom that transcends the financial realm, emphasizing the importance of perseverance and prioritizing one's health—a fragile 'glass ball' that, if dropped, can shatter. The program delved into diverse financial topics, equipping me with practical knowledge such as utilizing the Bloomberg Terminal, understanding mutual funds, discerning between buy-side and sell-side services, exploring insurance brokerage, comprehending asset fund investment strategies, navigating the short and futures marketplace, and unraveling the roles of analysts, managers, traders, and investors. We also explored the intricacies of stock ownership and the intriguing world of IPOs through terminal decoders. A highlight of the program was the National Stock Exchange visit, where I witnessed real-world trading and had the privilege of learning from industry leaders. Their insights were invaluable, reinforcing the significance of financial independence for every woman. The experience fueled my curiosity about investment strategies, company research, and the dynamic landscape of the financial world. Bloomberg Terminal became my gateway to exploring national and international equity, decoding securities, and launching in-depth research on companies. The program not only broadened my financial knowledge but also instilled in me the courage to forge ahead and cultivate a genuine interest in the financial domain. The interaction with the owner of the National Stock Exchange was a pinnacle moment, providing a rare glimpse into their experiences and adding a layer of real-world context to our learning. I left the program with newfound confidence, a deeper understanding of financial intricacies, and a strong motivation to continue exploring and growing in the financial world. In essence, the Apac Women in Finance program offered me an incredible learning journey, sparking my interest and providing me with a solid foundation in finance. My gratitude for this enriching experience knows no bounds."
