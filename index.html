About Us

APAC Women in Finance Initiative At Bloomberg, we’re committed to making the world of finance more accessible to women — using the tools of education, exposure and experience to inspire future generations and create a more inclusive global economy. Our new APAC Women In Finance initiative aims to connect 250 young women across five APAC countries to high-level role models who will provide them with insight into global finance with a focus on emerging fields such as ESG and fintech.
